Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom order take?

Custom orders typically take 2-3 weeks from confirmation to completion, depending on complexity and current order volume. We'll provide you with a timeline when you place your order.

How do I care for my crochet items?

Hand wash in cold water with mild detergent and lay flat to dry. This maintains the quality, shape, and texture of your handmade pieces for years to come.

When will workshops be available?

We're launching workshops in the coming months! Follow our social media or join our newsletter for updates on workshop dates and locations.

Can I request specific colors?

Absolutely! That's the beauty of custom orders. We work with a wide range of yarn colors and can match specific requests whenever possible.

Do you do gift wrapping?

Yes! All our gift sets include beautiful, sustainable packaging. We can also add personalized notes for special occasions.

What if I need a rush order?

Contact us directly at hello@tryb3.com for rush order requests. We'll do our best to accommodate based on our current schedule.

How do I measure for custom clothing?

We provide a detailed measurement guide when you place a custom clothing order. We can also guide you through the process via WhatsApp.
